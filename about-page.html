Who We're & Mission

Founded in 2017 in Canada, Vibing Tech strives to be the ultimate "One-Stop Digital Transformation Solution Provider" for local businesses throughout North America. Our dynamic portfolio aids organizations in streamlining their operations with efficient solutions like ERP, CRM, POS, PSA, business intelligence, FP&A, and more. We also offers comprehensive digital adoption services, including website development, mobile app development, and social media management. In Canada, We provides end-to-end managed services for your accounting, payroll, and recruitment processes. Give us a try, and you won't be disappointed. Our objective is to diminish inefficiencies and enhance productivity through end-to-end automation. Let's keep this 'About Us' brief, as we prioritize delivering results over making claims. As preferred partners of Odoo, Planful, and Oracle NetSuite, we specialize in business re-engineering services, adhering to an agile process involving business study, BRD, configuration, customization, integration, QA, training, consulting, and managed services.
